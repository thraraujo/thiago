Thiago Araujo

I am a theoretical physicist currently working at the Institute for Theoretical Physics in the University of Bern as a postdoc researcher.

I finished my Ph.D. at the Institute for Theoretical Physics in the Sao Paulo State University under the supervision of Horatiu Nastase. I also spent three years in South Korea as a postdoc at the Asia Pacific Center for Theoretical Physics.

Research

My research interests span the following topics: 2D integrable and conformal systems, the AdS/CFT correspondence and the construction of supergravity solutions. Representative papers are highlighted.
